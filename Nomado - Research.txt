European Union

1. eIDAS Regulation (Regulation (EU) No 910/2014)
- Legal framework for electronic identification and trust services across the EU.
- Explains which electronic IDs are recognized by public authorities and under what conditions.
- Source: https://eur-lex.europa.eu/eli/reg/2014/910/oj

2. GDPR (Regulation (EU) 2016/679)
- EU General Data Protection Regulation — governs how personal data can be collected, stored, and processed.
- Essential for any digital identity or credential system handling personal information.
- Source: https://eur-lex.europa.eu/eli/reg/2016/679/oj

3. European Health Insurance Card (EHIC) - European Commission
- Official EU page explaining the EHIC scheme, coverage, and use across EU/EEA/CH.
- Source: https://health.ec.europa.eu/system/files/2021-11/ehic_en_0.pdf

4. MyHealth@EU / European Health Data Space (European Commission)
- Describes EU efforts to enable cross-border health data exchange.
- Source: https://health.ec.europa.eu/ehealth-digital-health-and-care/european-health-data-space_en

Poland

5. Polish Act on Identity Cards (Ustawa o dowodach osobistych)
- National law defining official identity cards, issuance procedures, and legal validity.
- Shows that only state authorities can issue official IDs.
- Source: https://isap.sejm.gov.pl/isap.nsf/DocDetails.xsp?id=WDU20190000060

6. Polish Penal Code – Article 270 (Document Forgery)
- Criminal provision penalizing the creation, alteration, or use of false documents.
- Includes stricter penalties when the document is an ID or official certificate.
- Source: https://isap.sejm.gov.pl/isap.nsf/DocDetails.xsp?id=WDU19970880553

Bulgaria

7. Закон за българските лични документи (Personal Documents Act)
- Defines what counts as a Bulgarian personal document (passport, ID card, driving licence).
- Confirms these are state-issued and state property.
- Source: https://lex.bg/laws/ldoc/2134173441

8. Български Наказателен кодекс (Bulgarian Penal Code)
- Articles criminalizing document forgery and illegal possession or creation of identity documents.
- Source: https://lex.bg/laws/ldoc/1589654529

International & Comparative

9. UK – Forgery and Counterfeiting Act 1981
- UK statute that defines forgery offences and the illegal production or use of documents resembling official ones.
- Source: https://www.legislation.gov.uk/ukpga/1981/45

10. USA – 18 U.S. Code § 1028 (Fraud in connection with identification documents)
- Federal law prohibiting production or use of fake or unauthorized identification documents.
- Source: https://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/18/1028

Technical & Standards References

11. W3C Verifiable Credentials (VC) Standard
- Defines the data model for cryptographically verifiable digital credentials.
- Source: https://www.w3.org/TR/vc-data-model/

12. FHIR (Fast Healthcare Interoperability Resources)
- HL7 international standard for exchanging healthcare data electronically.
- Source: https://hl7.org/fhir/

13. SMART Health Cards (VCI Initiative)
- Standard for portable, verifiable vaccination and test result credentials.
- Used by Apple Wallet, Google Wallet, and CommonHealth.
- Source: https://smarthealth.cards/

14. ISO/IEC 18013-5:2021 (Mobile Driving Licence)
- Defines the international standard for digital/mobile driving licences and NFC verification.
- Source: https://www.iso.org/standard/69084.html

NFC and Security

15. NXP MIFARE DESFire EV3 Overview
- Example of a secure element (smartcard chip) used for encrypted NFC credentials.
- Shows hardware-level protections against cloning and skimming.
- Source: https://www.nxp.com/products/rfid-nfc/mifare-ics/mifare-desfire/mifare-desfire-ev3:MIFARE-DESFIRE-EV3

16. OWASP Cheat Sheet – Secure Storage and Data Protection
- Security best practices for protecting cryptographic keys and sensitive data.
- Source: https://cheatsheetseries.owasp.org/cheatsheets/Cryptographic_Storage_Cheat_Sheet.html

17. European Union Agency for Cybersecurity (ENISA) – Electronic ID Security Reports**
- Analyses of eID and smartcard security in the EU.
- Source: https://www.enisa.europa.eu/topics/identity-and-trust

19. Apple Health / Wallet (SMART Health Card integration)
- Apple’s official documentation on storing and presenting verifiable health data.
- Source: https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT212752

20. Google Wallet Health Pass Integration
- Google Wallet supports adding digital vaccination or test certificates.
- Source: https://developers.google.com/wallet/verticals/health-cards

- Global insurer app that allows policyholders to view and use their health coverage digitally.
- Source: https://www.allianzcare.com/en/myhealth-app.html
